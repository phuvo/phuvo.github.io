# Linear regression

## Definition

Prediction
$$
\hat{y} = f(x)
$$

Residual sum of squares (RSS)
$$
\operatorname{\small{RSS}} = \sum_{i=1}^n (y_i - \hat{y_i})^2
$$

Mean squared error (MSE) - also called L<sub>2</sub>
$$
\operatorname{\small{MSE}} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^n (y_i - \hat{y_i})^2
$$
